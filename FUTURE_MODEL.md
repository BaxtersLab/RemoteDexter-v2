# Future Model and Closure Transparency

RemoteDexter (RD) launches as fully open and auditable. Some components may become closed-source or encoded in the future. This will be disclosed clearly.

## Commitments
- The initial launch is fully open and auditable.
- No hidden behavior will be added retroactively without disclosure.
- Core sovereignty principles remain intact:
  - No call-home behavior
  - User-owned endpoints only
  - User-initiated connections

Any future closure will be clearly disclosed in release notes and documentation updates.

## If closed-source components are added later
- They will be layered on top of a known-good, audited base.
- They will not introduce hidden network behavior or backdoors.
- They will not change the bootstrap or pairing trust boundaries without disclosure.
